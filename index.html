SF HACKS

MARCH 18 to 19, 2017

APPLY

What's SF Hacks?

SF Hacks is San Francisco State University’s collegiate hackathon. Over 350 students from all over California will come together to learn, experiment, and create in 24 hours. SF Hacks is where your crazy ideas will become reality. It’s where you’re encouraged to do things that you’ve never done before: make an app, a website, a game, — anything.

FAQs

What's a hackathon?

A hackathon is where you turn your crazy ideas into real stuff. Hundreds of hackers from across California will gather and build something they're passionate about. You take care of building. We'll take care of you.


What's Major Leaque Hacking?

Major League Hacking (MLH) is the official student hackathon league. Each year, MLH powers over 200 weekend-long invention competitions that inspire innovation, cultivate communities and teach computer science skills to more than 65,000 students around the world. MLH is an engaged and passionate maker community, consisting of the next generation of technology leaders and entrepreneurs.

SF Hacks is an official MLH Member Event. We follow the MLH Code Of Conduct. You authorize us to share certain application/registration information for event administration, ranking, MLH administration, pre and post-event informational e-mails, and occasional messages about hackathons in line with the MLH Privacy Policy


How much does it cost?

SF Hacks is free for all admitted participants. We’ll have WiFi, food, workshops, and awesome swag during the entire 24 hours you’ll be with us. Travel reimbursement will be available for selected participants.


What if I don't have a team?

The hacker ethos is all about teamwork. If you don’t have a teammate, we’ll hold a team-formation workshop where you can meet your new best friend.


Who can attend?

SF Hacks is open to all majors. Whether you’re a budding ethnic studies, cinema, or computer science major, we want you to be here. Be prepared to learn, brainstorm, and build amazing software/hardware. Throughout the hackathon, we’ll be holding workshops where students can learn how to use various tools in developing websites and apps.


What can I make?

You can build anything you want, whether it’s a website, app, hardware, or tool.


What if I have more questions?

Please feel free to send us a message over on Facebook, Twitter, or send us an email at team@sfhacks.io

Where will SF Hacks be?

SF Hacks will take place at the Student Life Events Center (also known as the Annex 1) on the west side of San Francisco State University’s campus. The address is: 1 North State Drive, San Francisco CA, 94132

If you live in the Bay Area, you can take Bay Area Rapid Transit (BART) to Daly City and then take the MUNI 28 bus to SF State’s campus. For non-local students, we’ll be providing travel reimbursement (up to $50) for selected participants.
